Information About Our Data

The information produced from our functions comes from an economic World Bank data set and is used to produce several pieces of information from our code. World Bank is an organization that collects data around the world to be used for research and various other purposes. The World Bank operates under an Open Terms of use. This means that "Unless indicated otherwise in the data or indicator metadata, you are free to copy, distribute, adapt, display or include the data in other products for commercial or noncommercial purposes at no cost under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License."
