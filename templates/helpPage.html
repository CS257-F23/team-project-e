Help Page

This page is a resource for users to understand our website.

If you want to learn more about the statistics of a country, click on the "Home" tab in the navigation bar. This will lead you to the homepage where you can find more about the different statistics our website provides.

If you want to learn more about the dataset, click on the "Data" tab in the navigation bar. This will lead you to the datapage where you can find out where we got the data from.
