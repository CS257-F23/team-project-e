Welcome to our World Bank Financial Data Website!

There are 3 functionalities for this app.

You can either retrieve:

  • A summary of four interesting statistics about a country
  • A comparison between the percentage of country that has a financial account to the worldwide statistic
  • A comparison between the average age of a country and the percentage of the population that is worried about financing their education

You can use these statistics about various countries to learn about the financial and demographic status of a given country. By providing the comparison of these statistics across multiple countries globally, we provide invaluable data for researchers, professors, and students alike.
