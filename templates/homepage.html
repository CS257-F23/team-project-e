World Bank Financial Inclusion Data

There are 3 functionalities for this app.

You can either retrieve a summary of four interesting statistics about a country, compare the percentage of a country that has a financial account to the worldwide statistic, or compare the average age of a country to the percentage of the population that is worried about financing their education.You can use these statistics about various countries to learn about the financial and demographic status of a given country. By providing the comparison of these statistics across multiple countries globally, we provide invaluable data for researchers, professors, and students alike.
